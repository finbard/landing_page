Search Quotes

You can use our search page to receive quotes from all lenders on our platform.

These quotes are based on the data we analyse from your profiles. You can adjust your requirements for the loans based on your needs. Subsequently, quotes from lenders will be automatically updated.

  • Percentage of Revenue - amount of money you would like to borrow relative to your annual sales.
  • Upfront Fee - payable upfront instead of interest payments (to be deducted from loan).
  • Repayment Level - percentage of revenue that will go towards loan repayment.
