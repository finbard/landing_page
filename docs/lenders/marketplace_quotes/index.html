Potential Quotes

You will be able to provide quotes to our customers, when they are searching for new capital. These quotes will be based on the risk profile of our customers and your risk appetite.

Quotes will be generated automatically every time customers adjust their requirements for the loan, based on their needs.

  • Percentage of Revenue - amount of money you would like to borrow relative to your annual sales.
  • Upfront Fee - payable upfront instead of interest payments (to be deducted from loan).
  • Repayment Level - percentage of revenue that will go towards loan repayment.
