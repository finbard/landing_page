Analyse Quotes

Review each quote in detail to understand if it fits your business needs.

To help you compare the quotes we also provide an estimated NPV. This value is based on predicted "return on investment" if all capital is spent on marketing. All predictions are based on your historical data, hence the importance of connecting all your profiles.

  • Marketing Costs (Red) - assumed daily spend on marketing using the capital borrowed.
  • Predicted Sales (Green) - predicted sales based on additional marketing expenditure and historic marketing performance.
  • Loan Repayments (Blue) - percentage of sales that goes towards loan repayment, depending on agreed repayment levels.
